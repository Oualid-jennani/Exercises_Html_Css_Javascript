Nanatsu No Taizai (zeldris)

Who is Zeldres ?

Zeldris「ゼルドリス」 is an elite warrior of the Demon
directly under the Demon King as the Piety of the Ten
Commandments.He is the younger brother of the series main
protagonist, Meliodas, as well as the youngest son of the
Demon King.He is also the Demon King's representative
and executioner. Visit Nanatsu No Taizai Website

Specification of Waleed

Zeldris is a short yet muscular young man. He greatly resembles his brother Meliodas, though with a few slight differences. His hair is spikey black and grows counterclockwise to Meliodas' and they both have the same demon mark, except his is over his left eye instead of his right. His full demon mark is similar to his older brothers when he was the leader of the Ten Commandments. His attire is a body suit, crimson in color and has a white insignia on several places. The suit has a open collar and exposes his chest. He has silver gauntlets and carries his sword with a strap on his hip.
